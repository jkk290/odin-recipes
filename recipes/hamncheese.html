Ham and Cheese Sandwich

Description

This ham and cheese sandwich is great on a cool night with a cup of tomato soup. I add spinach, arugula, tomatoes, roasted peppers, chopped olives, artichokes, or a spoonful of salsa to the sandwich, depending on my mood and what's in the fridge.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slies of bread
  • butter
  • Swiss cheese
  • Deli ham
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat a skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Spread one side fo each slice of bread with butter.
  4. Place on slice butter-side down onto hot skillet. Top with Swiss cheese and ham.
  5. Spread the unbuttered side of second slice of bread with mayo and mustard. Place it butter-side up on sandwich.
  6. Cook in hot skillet unitl golden brown and cheese is melted. About 3 minutes per side.
